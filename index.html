Where Every
Piece Begins

Devji was not built on import catalogues or mass production lines. It was built on craft — the kind that begins in Bahrain, moves through the hands of skilled artisans, and ends as something you hand down.

Gold jewellery is our foundation. Handcrafted, considered, and made to last beyond the occasion it marks. Across the GCC, families have walked into a Devji store at the most significant moments of their lives — an engagement, a wedding, a birth, a milestone — and walked out with something that holds that memory.

That tradition is coming to Kuwait.

"Handcrafted gold jewellery is not made in a hurry. The pace of the work is part of what makes it worth keeping."

Kuwait has always been part of our world. The Gulf's discerning buyers — Kuwaiti families, expatriate communities, those who know the difference between gold that is made and gold that is manufactured — have long been our kind of client.

The Avenues Mall boutique is a consultation space. Appointments are how we work here — because handcrafted jewellery deserves a conversation, not a counter.

I.

Jewellery Consultation

You have something in mind — a style, an occasion, a feeling. Bring it. Our consultants work through the handcrafted gold collection with you. Ninety minutes. One room. Your full attention.

What You Leave With

The right piece, or the confidence to return when ready.

II.

Bridal Consultation

A bridal set is not a purchase. It is a decision. We take every piece — ring, earrings, necklace, bangles — one at a time, in one sitting. Bring whoever needs to be present.

What You Leave With

A confirmed selection or a detailed shortlist, ready for the next conversation.

III.

Custom Design

An idea in your head that does not exist in any collection. Describe it. Sketch it. Show us a reference. Our team takes your brief back to Bahrain and returns with a design made entirely for you.

What You Leave With

A design brief, a timeline, and a piece no one else owns.
