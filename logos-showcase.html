Usage Notes
Option 01 — Best For

Website Nav & App Icons

The gem mark works as a standalone favicon or app icon. The full lockup works well in the website header and on all collateral.

Option 02 — Best For

Print, Signage & Packaging

No icon dependency. The typographic treatment holds strong at large sizes — shopping bags, store fascia, certificates.

Colour System

Gold Always. Text Adapts.

Gold (#C9A84C) is fixed across all uses. Text is cream (#F2E8D0) on dark, near-black (#1A1410) on light.
